What is PrismMTR?

PrismMTR is a dedicated Minecraft Transit Railway launcher optimized for Fabric 1.20.1. MTR stands for Minecraft Transit Railway - a mod that adds realistic trains, railway systems, and transit infrastructure to Minecraft. PrismMTR provides stability, performance optimization, and seamless mod management for the best MTR experience.

How do I install PrismMTR?

Go to the Download page, sign in with GitHub, Google, or Discord, then click the Download button. Run the installer and follow the on-screen prompts. The launcher comes pre-configured for MTR on Fabric 1.20.1.

Why do I need to connect Discord?

Discord connection is required for support tickets so our team can contact you directly. It also enables priority support, community features, and allows you to receive notifications about your projects and account.

What is a Minecraft nickname for?

Your Minecraft nickname is used for whitelist functionality on our community servers and to identify you in-game. It must match your actual Minecraft username exactly (case-sensitive).

How do I add mods to my instance?

PrismMTR comes pre-configured with the Minecraft Transit Railway mod and recommended dependencies. To add additional mods, open the launcher, select your instance, and use the built-in mod browser. All mods are verified for Fabric 1.20.1 compatibility.

Is PrismMTR free to use?

Yes! PrismMTR is completely free and open source. It's built by the community for the Minecraft Transit Railway community.

What are the system requirements?

PrismMTR requires Windows 10+, macOS 10.14+, or a modern Linux distribution. You'll need at least 4GB of RAM (8GB recommended), Java 17+, and a graphics card with OpenGL 4.4 support. The launcher will automatically manage Java installations for you.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to your Dashboard, navigate to Settings, and scroll to the bottom. Click "Delete Account" and confirm. This action is permanent and will remove all your data, projects, and company memberships.
